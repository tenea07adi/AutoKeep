Privacy Policy

Welcome to AutoKeep! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app. AutoKeep is designed to help you store and manage schedules for your vehicles, such as oil change dates. All data is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or share your data.

Information We Collect

AutoKeep does not collect or store any personal information or data on external servers. All data you input, such as vehicle schedules, is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

The data you provide is used solely for the purpose of managing your vehicle schedules. Since all data is stored locally, it is not accessed, processed, or used by us in any other way.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your data with any third parties. Since your data is stored locally on your device, it is not transmitted to any external servers or services.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. We recommend using your device's security features, such as passwords or biometric authentication, to protect your data.

User Rights

As all data is stored locally:

  • You have full control over your data.
  • You can delete or modify your data at any time through the app.
  • If you uninstall the app, all data stored locally will be deleted.

Third-Party Services

AutoKeep does not use any third-party services that collect or process your data.

Children's Privacy

AutoKeep is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@autokeep.com.
